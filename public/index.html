    What's Yummy Finder?

  • Thank you for visiting the Yummy Finder Website! This website uses Spoonacular API , and you can search recipes after your preferences, such as gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegetarian/vegan! Once you make an account with this website, you can save your favorite recipes and even make a shopping list! Enjoy your food journey with Yummy Finder!

    How do you search recipes?

    Show More

    expand_more

  • With the recipes you see on the page, you can filter them depending on what kind of recipes you want to see! You can choose a vegetarian/vegan option, gluten-free, dairy-free option. And also you can find very healthy recipes. If you want to see more recipes, just click the 'new recipes' button.
